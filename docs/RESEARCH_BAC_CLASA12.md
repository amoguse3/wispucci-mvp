# ANCE BAC (Clasa 12) — Massive Research: real vs umanist

> Deep pass over the real national Bacalaureat exams (ciclul liceal), sessions 2024-2025 (sesiune de bază, suplimentară, pretestare). The defining fact of BAC is the **profile split**: a student sits a different exam depending on whether they're **real** (științe reale / sport) or **umanist** (științe umaniste / arte). Same subject name, different program, different test, different barem. The KB and graph must be **profile-aware** or the tutor will teach the wrong material.

---

## The profile system (this is the core insight)

| | Profil REAL (+ sport) | Profil UMANIST (+ arte) |
|---|---|---|
| Math exam | **M-real**: harder, includes calculus | **M-uman**: no calculus, more stats/combinatorics |
| History | broader universal + national, essay | more depth on humanities themes, essay |
| Both | 180 min, 100 points | 180 min, 100 points |

Every liceu student takes: **Limba română**, **Matematică** (or History for some tracks), a **profile discipline** (disciplina de profil), and a **chosen discipline** (disciplina la solicitare). The math/history profile variant is the make-or-break difference.

## PART 1 — MATHEMATICS (real vs uman, confirmed from 2025 baremes)

### Structure
- 180 min, 100 points.
- **M-real: 12 items.** Partial credit per step (same philosophy as gimnaziu but far deeper).
- **M-uman: 14 items.** More, smaller items; no calculus.

### M-REAL topic inventory (from sb25 / ss25 baremes)
- Powers & radicals, algebraic fractions (rationalization).
- **Logarithms** (log properties, log equations).
- **Trigonometry**: equations on an interval, tg/sin/cos identities.
- **Complex numbers** (modulus, operations).
- Inequalities, sign tables (curba semnelor), rational inequalities.
- **Sequences** (monotonicity, recurrence).
- **Calculus — the real-only block:**
  - **Limits** (lim x→0, factoring, indeterminate forms).
  - **Derivatives** (derivative of terms, f'(0), tangent).
  - **Integrals** (primitives, Newton-Leibniz formula, definite integrals).
- **Solid geometry**: cylinder (radius/height/volume), pyramid (dihedral angle, inscribed circle of rhombus base), trapezoid areas via similar triangles.
- Combinatorics / probability (item 11-12: C(n,k), arrangements).

### M-UMAN topic inventory (from ss25 barem, 14 items)
- Logarithms (log27, log properties).
- Powers with negative/fractional exponents.
- **Determinants** (2×2, rule of calculation).
- Complex numbers (modulus).
- Quadratic conditions (discriminant, sign conditions).
- Similar figures, ratio equations.
- Solid geometry: cone (lateral area, generatoare), prism (diagonal, base area, volume), cylinder.
- Function value / point membership.
- Rational inequalities.
- Sequences / arithmetic progression (systems on terms).
- **Statistics**: ordering a series, **median**, mean (item 14).
- **Combinatorics**: factorials (7! / (3!·4!)), probability.
- **NO calculus** (no limits, derivatives, integrals). This is the single biggest divergence from real.

### Barem logic (both profiles)
- Any valid method reaching the answer = full marks; no shown work required unless the item asks.
- Points awarded per stage: set up → transform → solve → answer. Integer points only.
- **Teach:** for real, calculus items are point-dense (item 10 worth 8p across limit/derivative/integral parts). For uman, stats + combinatorics are free points if drilled.

## PART 2 — HISTORY (Istoria românilor și universală, real vs uman)

### Structure (both profiles, from pretestare 2025)
- 180 min, 100 points, **4 subiecte**:
  - **Subiectul I (24 p):** source dossier A-B, 5 items (explain term, name factors, explain context, compare authors' opinions, appreciate impact).
  - **Subiectul al II-lea (20 p):** single source, ~5 items (explain, select, determine author's opinion + argue, formulate consequence, appreciate a personality's role).
  - **Subiectul al III-lea (21 p):** map analysis, 3 items (identify event/process, determine context, formulate consequences), all with argumentation.
  - **Subiectul al IV-lea (35 p):** full one-page essay from sources A-C.
- Same skeleton for real and uman; **uman items carry deeper point ladders** (e.g. Sub. I item 4 worth 7p on uman vs 2p slots on real) and reach further into pre-modern history.

### Essay barem (Subiectul IV, 35p) — the big scorer
Explicit dimensions, each separately scored:
- ≥3 relevant, scientifically correct arguments — up to 10p.
- ≥2 cause-effect relations — up to 6p.
- critical integration of the sources — up to 4p.
- placement in time and space — up to 4p.
- mention a relevant historical personality — up to 2p.
- logical plan (intro/body/conclusion) — up to 7p.
- correct historical language — up to 2p.

**Teach the essay as a checklist**: 3 arguments + 2 cause-effect links + source citations + dates/places + a personality + intro/body/conclusion + clean terminology. Students who write prose without hitting these boxes leave 35 points on the table.

### History topic inventory across real + uman tests
- **Antiquity / pre-modern:** Roman conquest of Dacia, romanization (real pr25); Mircea cel Bătrân, Alexandru cel Bun, medieval Țările Române (uman pr25); Iulius Caesar, triumvirate, Roman forms of government (uman pr25).
- **Modern:** Marile descoperiri geografice (real pr25 essay); Războiul ruso-turc 1877-78, Carol I, independence of Romania (real pr25).
- **20th century:** territorial losses 1940, Molotov-Ribbentrop, Dictatul de la Viena, Tratatul de la Craiova; **Romania in WWII** (campania din Est/Vest, 23 August 1944, entry into Soviet sphere) — uman pr25 essay.
- Recurring across both: source comparison, cause-effect essays, map analysis, personality appreciation.
